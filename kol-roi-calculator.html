The 5-Layer Attribution Model

Most Web3 teams measure KOL campaigns at the reach or engagement layer and stop there. That's why ROI feels like a black box. Wevolv3 measures five layers, end-to-end:

  • Layer 1 — Reach: total unique accounts exposed (followers × impressions multiplier).
  • Layer 2 — Engagement: likes, replies, reposts / impressions.
  • Layer 3 — Click: actual clickthrough to your CTA (tracked links, UTMs).
  • Layer 4 — Wallet: wallet connections / on-chain signups attributed to the click.
  • Layer 5 — Retention: 30-day+ active wallets — the only metric that matters for token health.

Defaults are conservative mid-market benchmarks for Crypto Twitter KOLs (50K–100K followers). Adjust to your own historical data for a realistic forecast.
